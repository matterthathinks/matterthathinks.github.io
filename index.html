About this place
Substack RSS Feed Contact

This is a place where I think out loud. About economics, law, power, science — and whatever else refuses to leave my head.

I'm Ashutosh Nayak, an Integrated M.Sc. Physics student at NISER Bhubaneswar in my final year. I consume a lot — videos, articles, books, ideas — and I can't stop trying to connect things. This is where those connections end up.

The writing here will change as I change. Right now it's earnest and a little raw. That's fine. A small part of me hopes it stays that way.

Everything is free. No algorithm selects what appears here. No advertiser shapes it.
