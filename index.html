About this place
Substack RSS Feed Contact

This is a blog about things that matter — matter that thinks, equations that feel, ideas that resist easy summary. Written from NISER Bhubaneswar, between problem sets and experiments.

I write about physics (radiation, measurement, the stubborn weirdness of quantum mechanics), philosophy (how we know what we know, and why that's harder than it looks), and the examined life — what growing up inside science actually feels like from the inside.

The blog will change as I change. Right now it is earnest and exploratory. It may become sharper, stranger, or quieter. That seems right for writing done honestly over time.

No algorithm selects what appears here. No advertiser shapes it. Just writing, as carefully as I can manage.
