About this place
Substack RSS Feed Email

This is a place where I think out loud. About economics, science, politics, culture — and whatever else refuses to leave my head.

I'm Ashutosh Nayak, an Integrated M.Sc. Physics student at NISER Bhubaneswar. I consume a lot — videos, articles, books, ideas — and I can't stop trying to connect things. This is where those connections end up.

The writing here will change as I change. Right now it's earnest and a little raw. That's fine. A small part of me hopes it stays that way.

Everything is free. No algorithm selects what appears here. No advertiser shapes it.

Essays by email, when they're ready.

No schedule, no noise. Just a note when something new is worth your time.
